Welcome to Sunnydale High School

Sunnydale High School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing students to achieve their full potential. We offer a diverse range of programs and extracurricular activities to support student growth and development.

About Us

Established in 1990, Sunnydale High School has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence and community involvement. Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive learning environment for all students.

Admissions

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds. Please visit our admissions page for more information on how to apply and the necessary requirements.

Academics

Our academic programs are designed to challenge and inspire students. We offer a variety of subjects and advanced placement courses to cater to different interests and career paths.
